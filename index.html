MANJIRA

ABOUT

Manjira is a branding and packaging project that reimagines traditional Indian bangles for a younger audience. While bangles hold deep cultural and emotional significance, their presentation often fails to reflect this richness. This project explores how thoughtful design, storytelling, and a maximalist visual approach can make bangles feel expressive, celebratory, and relevant today. Through bold colors, layered graphics, and playful details, Manjira bridges tradition with contemporary design sensibilities.
